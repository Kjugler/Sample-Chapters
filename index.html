Sample Chapters – The Agnes Protocol

CHAPTER 1

The snow hadn’t stopped. It was the kind of storm that made people cancel plans, call in sick, or light a fire and disappear for a while. But Fred Palmer wasn’t most people.

He adjusted the knot of his tie and checked the time. 2:03 a.m.

Perfect.

Across the street, a dark window flickered. A signal. The game was on.

Fred smiled. “Let’s get to work.”
